About Mahakumbh

The Mahakumbh is one of the world's most significant spiritual events, held every 12 years at the sacred confluence of the Ganga, Yamuna, and Saraswati rivers. Millions gather to experience spiritual awakening, cleanse their sins, and attain moksha (liberation). With roots in ancient Vedic traditions, the Mahakumbh is a celebration of unity, devotion, and enlightenment. The Kumbh Mela is one of the largest religious gatherings in the world, celebrated by millions of devotees who come together to take part in spiritual rituals, holy dips, and prayers. In 2025, the Maha Kumbh Mela will take place in Prayagraj (Allahabad), Uttar Pradesh.
